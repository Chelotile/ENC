Exploring the spatiality of Airbnb Rentals and the Urban Environment 

Project Introduction


Airbnb is an internet-based peer-to-peer accommodation platform that allows people to earn money by renting out their houses. Despite its success, Airbnb has caused negative impacts such as depriving locals of access to a pool of previously available residences due to mortgage and rent prices increase and decreasing real estate values and hotel revenues. Due to potential negative impacts, it is critical to understand the spatial association between the neighborhood environment and Airbnb rentals for effective legislative measures. Previous spatial research on Airbnb has made valuable contributions to the study, however, they tend to have limitations in terms of models and variables used for the study. Due to this limitation, researchers have suggested the inclusion of locational characteristics in the study, the use of advanced models, and conducting the study in other areas.

Study Objectives

  • To analyze the spatiotemporal pattern of Airbnb rentals.
  • To built a predictive model for Airbnb rentals price using regression models.
  • To analyze the spatial association of Airbnb rentals and the urban environment.
